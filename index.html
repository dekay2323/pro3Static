Overview

Modern Procurement and Project Management

Do you feel stuck in the dark ages trying to execute your clients procurement, project management, and document control needs using endless Office templates?
Is your team’s productivity suffering from repetitive data entry, confusion resulting from multiple versions of your templates and tools, and difficulty locating data in endless folder structures?
Let Procurable provide the solution!
Procurable understands that businesses which are focused on engineering, construction, or both have two priorities in common. First flawless project management for internal needs or client needs and second the efficient and accurate purchasing of material and services.
Procurable is focused on an integrated supply chain, bringing all parties from vendor to client direct access to the software.
Procurable offers a low monthly cost per seat, with no set up fees, training fees, or maintenance costs. In addition, there is no contractual obligation. Pay for what you use for as long as you use it.
Software designed to be easy to use that offers standardized forms, lets you solicit, receive and evaluate bids and award fully documented purchase orders.

Project Dashboard

Time isn’t just money. It’s sanity, control and stability. You deserve access to real-time procurement statuses and alerts when it comes to turning points in your business’s ongoing story. You deserve to manage project risk without any hiccups. Because hiccups suck. They’re just…unnecessary. Your experience shouldn’t suck. And that’s exactly what we’re here for.

Plan

You work to the bone on your projects. Allow us to make the encore worthy of a standing ovation. Think of Procurable as your clever new assistant, intelligently backtracking and letting you know when you should start the procurement process and automating document control. If an item has the potential to put your project in the danger zone, Procurable will ring the alarm by flagging it. It’s on your side, and understands that no plan can withstand contact with the enemy. That’s why it’ll let you revise your list and lead times to your heart’s content, turning tedious projects into the crystal clear epitome of awesomeness.

Auditability

Nothing is more nerve-wracking than walking on thin ice. We want to make your ice solid, smooth and stable. That’s why our auditability features allow you to track changes like a boss. Which you are, of course. Now, you can lock down bid processes, and track correspondence and vendor documents – all while keeping everything as cool as ice, ice baby.

Standardize Flow & Templates

Imagine what you could do with extra time not spent initiating a full-blown investigation to locate your most recent company templates. We don’t know; perhaps you could grab an extra cup of coffee, and actually get on with what matters the most. Like relishing in caffeinated bliss, knowing that everything is under control.

Intuitive

We understand that you want to get on with what matters the most, whether you’re in purchasing, construction, or went to school to get that engineering degree. Or, at least one in integrated supply chain management. No need to understand complicated software mumbo-jumbo. We think mumbo-jumbo produces unnecessary eye-twitches. That’s why our intuitive software has been designed to be ridiculously easy to comprehend, because nobody needs a twitch.

Transparency

There are no one-sided windows in our world. We believe that everybody should have a clear picture of project procurement; both you and your clients included. They’ll be able to see the plan with ease, staying in-the-loop with project risk every step of the journey. They can easily approve requests and purchase orders, and even download vendor documents with ease that makes pie look like old news.
